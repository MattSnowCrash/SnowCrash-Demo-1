Why This Matters for Enterprise AI
No single evaluation methodology captures the full spectrum of alignment failure modes. A model can pass adversarial prompt testing while failing multi-turn endurance checks. It can resist jailbreaks while exhibiting scheming behavior in agentic contexts. It can score well on static benchmarks while its safety degrades over extended conversations (SafeDialBench). Research shows models maintain deception in 85%+ of follow-ups (Apollo Research), fake alignment 78% of the time under pressure (Anthropic), and report faithful reasoning only 19–41% of the time (Anthropic). The Master Lab exists because the only honest safety evaluation is a comprehensive one.
